In order to evaluate whether my additional department-specific FE controls are necessary for estimating treatment effects, I have ran my regressions four times with increasing degrees of department-specific FEs. First with only the department FE, then adding department-year FEs, adding department-month FEs, and finally adding department-holiday FEs. \\

As shown below, it appears as though the department-year FEs are important to the estimation, but the additional FEs beyond that don't appear to make substantial differences. \\

Dr Bates - to explain the graph formatting, there are three trios of graph panels. Each panel has the same rows with (Dept FEs, Adding Dept-Year, Adding Dept-Month, Adding Dept-Holiday). To avoid overwhelming you with an increible amount of graphs, I have only done this for three outcomes (Stop Share, Search Rate, and Hit Rate), and for three of my general specifications (Main, Dropping T-1, and Balanced). \\
