\section{Determining Event and Estimation Window}

Due to imperfect reporting data across this study's observation period, I need to determine how many 15-day bins to plot - what I am calling my event window. I constructed a k-density plot of nonempty department-event 15-day bins, as shown, and have chosen to use 7 bins (+/-105 days) before and after the death-event for my main event study plots. \\

In order to maintain as much of the usable data as possible, I construct an estimation window where department-date observations outside of +/- 105 days and inside of +/- 240 days will be kept and used in my main estimations. \\
