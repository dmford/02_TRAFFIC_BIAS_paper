Noting unusual T-1 stop volumes, I looked into which departments are driving the unusual pattern. To this end, I plot numbers of stops in the preperiod with the average T-1 and T-7 through T-2 volumes overlaid. \\

First is the South Carolina Highway Patrol, which reported a very high number of stops during the week of July 4th, suggesting that my July 3rd through July 5th indicator variables may be insufficient to capture the abnormal holiday behaviors. \\
