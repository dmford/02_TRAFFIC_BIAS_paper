In what follows, Panel A follows more traditional Diff-in-Diff and Triple-Diff structures, while Panel B utilizes a variation of the Mundlak Device from Wooldridge (2021), stemming from Mundlak (1978). Equation \ref{eq1_DiD} below is what I am calling ``Panel A: Diff-in-Diff'', equation \ref{eq3_DiDiD} ``Panel B: Triple Difference'', equation \ref{eq5_TWM_DiD} ``Panel A: TWM Diff-in-Diff'', and equation \ref{eq6_TWM_DiDiD} ``Panel B: TWM Triple Difference''. \\

%	Equation #1, Difference in Differences: 
\begin{equation}
	\begin{split}
		Y^{o}_{d,t} = \beta Post_{d,t} + \boldsymbol{X'}_{d,t} \boldsymbol{\gamma} + \boldsymbol{\theta}_{dy} + \boldsymbol{\eta}_{ym} + u_{d,t} 
	\label{eq1_DiD}
	\end{split}
\end{equation}

% 	Equation #2, Event Study: 
\begin{equation}
	\begin{split}
		Y^{o}_{d,t} = \delta_{1} D^{k\in[-26,-15]}_{d,t} + \sum_{k=-14(1)13} \beta_{k}D^{k}_{d,t} + \\ 
		\delta_{2} D^{k\in[14,25]}_{d,t} + \boldsymbol{\theta}_{dy} + \boldsymbol{\eta}_{ym} + u_{d,t}
            \label{eq2_estudy}
        \end{split}
\end{equation}

%	Equation #3, Triple Difference: 
\begin{equation} 
	\begin{split}
		Y^{o}_{d,t} = \beta_{1} Post_{d,t} + \beta_{2} Post_{d,t}*Scr_{d,t} + \boldsymbol{X'}_{d,t} \boldsymbol{\gamma} + \boldsymbol{\theta}_{dy} + \boldsymbol{\eta}_{ym} + u_{d,t} 
	\label{eq3_DiDiD}
	\end{split}
\end{equation}

% 	Equation #4, Event Study + Interaction: 
\begin{equation}
	\begin{split}
		Y^{o}_{d,t} = \delta_{1} D^{k\in[-26,-15]}_{d,t} + \sum_{k=-14(1)13} \beta_{k}D^{k}_{d,t} + \delta_{2} D^{k\in[14,25]}_{d,t} + \\
		\delta_{3} D_{d,t}*Scr_{d,t}^{k\in[-26,-15]} + \sum_{k=-14(1)13} \Gamma_{k}D^{k}_{d,t}*Scr_{d,t} + \\
		\delta_{4} D_{d,t}*Scr_{d,t}^{k\in[14,25]} + \boldsymbol{\theta}_{dy} + \boldsymbol{\eta}_{ym} + u_{d,t}
	\label{eq4_estudy_int}
	\end{split}
\end{equation}

%	Equation #1, TWM Difference in Differences: 
\begin{equation}
	\begin{split}
		Y^{o}_{d,t} = \beta Post_{d,t} + \boldsymbol{X'}_{d,t} \boldsymbol{\gamma} + \boldsymbol{\bar{X}}_{ym} \boldsymbol{\theta}_1 + \boldsymbol{\bar{X}}_{d} \boldsymbol{\zeta}_2 + \\ Post_{d,t} (\boldsymbol{\bar{X}}_{ym} - \boldsymbol{\bar{X}}) + Post_{d,t} (\boldsymbol{\bar{X}}_{d} - \boldsymbol{\bar{X}}) + u_{d,t} 
	\label{eq5_TWM_DiD}
	\end{split}
\end{equation}

%	Equation #3, TWM Triple Difference: 
\begin{equation} 
	\begin{split}
		Y^{o}_{d,t} = \beta_1 Post_{d,t} + \beta_2 Post_{d,t}*Scr + \boldsymbol{X'}_{d,t} \boldsymbol{\gamma} + \boldsymbol{\bar{X}}_{ym} \boldsymbol{\zeta}_1 + \boldsymbol{\bar{X}}_{d} \boldsymbol{\zeta}_2+ \\ Post_{d,t} (\boldsymbol{\bar{X}}_{ym} - \boldsymbol{\bar{X}}) + Post_{d,t} (\boldsymbol{\bar{X}}_{d} - \boldsymbol{\bar{X}}) + u_{d,t} 
	\label{eq6_TWM_DiDiD}
	\end{split}
\end{equation}

My data is aggregated to the department-day level, while I use department-event and year-month fixed effects. \\

To highlight my coefficients of interest, let $\boldsymbol{X'}_{d,t}$ be the $\boldsymbol{X}_{d,t}$ vector without the $Post_{d,t}$ dummy variable.
