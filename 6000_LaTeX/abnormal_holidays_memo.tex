Despite using a categorical holidays control, a few of the larger statewide departments still show substantial deviations from their typical policing behaviors. Most notably are the TX and OH State Police, as well as SC to a lesser extent. \\

Two notable things about the below: one, not all holidays exhibit these deviations. Two, a few stray stop dates surrounding my holiday controls have still snuck through. Further inspection will be needed to determine if the holiday definitions need to be expanded for all departments, or only for these select few. \\
