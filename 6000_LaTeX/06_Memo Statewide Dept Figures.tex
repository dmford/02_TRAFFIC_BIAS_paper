\author{David Ford}
\maketitle

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

Fixed effects for department, year, department-year, month, day of week, and a vector of holidays. \\

Additional indicators for a few abnormal reporting periods: a NOLA Bourbon Street shooting, the 2016 Philadelphia DNC, and anomalous reporting periods in Florida and Washington states. \\

Errors clustered at the department level. Pedestrian stops, plain view searches, and consent searches dropped. \\

The first third of figures are from my main sample, the second from a balanced sample in which treated departments have at least one observation in each 14-day bin, and the last from a uniform sample used across all outcomes of ~120,000 department-days. \\
